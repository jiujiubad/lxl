Hope it’s the last night of firework extravaganza in this year

by xiaolai on 2012/02/06 · 11 comments

in Daily

The noise of firework combined with false alarms of cars almost killed me. Today is “the 15th day of the 1st lunar month”, it should be quiet from tomorrow.

In January 29th, a report from Beijing’s municipal health department said one dead, two amputations and 223 other injuries from wayward fireworks.

And now is one week later, who knows the exact number of injuries?

According to the Globe and Mail,

“Some 183.7 tons of red paper scraps, leftovers from firework casings, have littered city streets like confetti, according to the Beijing Environmental Sanitation Engineering Group, requiring more than 5,000 street sweepers to clean up – though that was, reportedly, three tons less than last year’s mess.

…Tongren Hospital, the city’s leading ophthalmology hospital, put 14 doctors and surgeons on duty in its emergency room to handle an expected surge in eye injuries.”
