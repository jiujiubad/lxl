in 书——不得不看, 看看电影

时代(Time.com)制作了一个过去十年中最好作品列表。仔细看了一下，除了十部电影中大部分看过之外，电视剧、书籍列表中的基本上都没看过。于是，决定把电视剧中The Wire, Deadwood这两部HBO的作品补上（题外话：imdb也疯了，晕）；而书籍中，搜索了一下，在Gigapedia上可以找到其中的绝大多数。收获最大的是知道了Kazuo Ishiguro这个日裔英文作者（入榜书籍是他2005年出版的“Never Let Me Go）。查了一下Wikipedia，此人被评价为英语世界里最知名的作家之一。

Kazuo Ishiguro OBE (Japanese: カズオ・イシグロ (Kazuo Ishiguro) or 石黒 一雄 (Ishiguro Kazuo); born 8 November 1954) is a British novelist. He was born in Nagasaki, Japan, and his family moved to England in 1960. Ishiguro obtained his Bachelor’s degree from University of Kent in 1978 and his Master’s from the University of East Anglia‘s creative writing course in 1980. He became a British citizen in 1982.[1]

Ishiguro is one of the most celebrated contemporary fiction authors in the English speaking world, having received four Man Booker Prize nominations, including winning the 1989 prize for his novel The Remains of the Day.
