I’d love to introduce danwei.com to readers, an informative website about media, internet and urban life in China. It started in 2003, maintained by a team including Jeremy Goldkorn (blog,Â Twitter); Joel Martinsen (blog) and Eric Mu.

What is Danwei?
Danwei is a website about media, advertising and urban life in China.

With frequent reference to and translations from Mainland Chinese media, we publish fresh information about China that you won’t find anywhere else. We also produce original video shows and audio podcasts about China.

Using extensive Chinese language sources, we keeps tabs on a wide variety of subjects including legal and business stories, media and entertainment gossip, and the environment.

The Chinese word ‘Danwei’ (åä½) means ‘unit’, as in a unit of currency or measurement, or as in ‘work unit’ – the old term for a state-owned company that was supposed to provide cradle-to-grave employment, housing and medical treatment.
