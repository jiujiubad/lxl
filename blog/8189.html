xyran同学来信发给我《Justice – What’s the right thing to do》全部总计12集的字幕文件，是xml格式的：

  <Item from="72.49" duration="6.0699999999999994"><![CDATA[Not only that, he believes that  
those fundamental rights include]]></Item>
  <Item from="79.52" duration="6.8999999999999995"><![CDATA[a natural right to life, 
liberty, and property,]]></Item>
  <Item from="89.22999999999999" duration="5.8"><![CDATA[ and furthermore he argues
that the right to property]]></Item>
  <Item from="97.28" duration="5.05"><![CDATA[is not just the creation 
of government or of law.]]></Item>
  <Item from="102.89999999999999" duration="4.21"><![CDATA[The right to property is 
a natural right in the sense ]]></Item>
